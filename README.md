kids2 App
A site that does a search for kids activities based on different categories such as Sports, Educational, Performance and Volunteer. This is an app that provides a platform for both businesses and non profits to post their events. This app aims to serve all household income connecting families with lower income to different organizations that provide financial aid and lower costs or free activities.
This app also provides options for families wanting to give back to the community by filtering on Volunteer events appropriate for kids of various age range.
